# Shanghai

Nihao 👋

Shanghai is one of the most exciting cities I've ever visited. Here's what made my trip unforgettable:

## Top Attractions

- **Disneyland** - An amazing experience with unique Chinese elements mixed with classic Disney magic
- **Best dessert in town** - Don't miss the local bakeries near The Bund

## Travel Tips

The city is incredibly modern with excellent public transportation. The metro system is easy to navigate even if you don't speak Chinese.

### Food Scene

The street food is absolutely incredible. Make sure to try:
- Xiaolongbao (soup dumplings)
- Shengjianbao (pan-fried buns)
- Local pastries from the French Concession area

## Final Thoughts

Shanghai exceeded all my expectations. The blend of traditional Chinese culture with ultra-modern architecture creates a unique atmosphere you won't find anywhere else.
